Text Utilities

These are the various types of Text Utilities that Super UI provides .

Heading Utility

This will style your heading components . Only you have to use classes like h1, h2, h3 ,h4 ,h5.

Headline 1

Headline 2

Headline 3

Headline 4

Headline 5 

                
<h1 class="h1 m2">Headline 1 </h1>
<h2 class="h2 m2">Headline 2 </h2>
<h3 class="h3 m2">Headline 3 </h3>
<h4 class="h4 m2">Headline 4 </h4>
<h5 class="h5 m2">Headline 5 </h5>

Small Text

This will make your text smaller , you have to just add class txt--sm in your p tag .

This is a small Text . 


    
<p class="txt--sm">This is a small Text . </p>

Grey Text

This will make your text grey, you have to just add class txt--grey in your p tag .

This is a grey Text 

            
<p class="txt--grey"> This is a grey Text </p>

Center Text

This will make your text in center , you have to just add class txt--center in your p tag .

This Text is in Center . 


    
<p class="txt--center">This Text is in Center . </p>
