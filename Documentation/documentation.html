Introduction

Super UI is a open source component library , That is made to make your Life easy , and help you by saving your precious time . We have done all the hardwork , But stick with us we will make new features coming very soon .

Installation Guide

You can use our whole component library by just adding simple link given below . Just add this Link to your head tag of your HTML . 



<link rel="stylesheet" href="https://superui.netlify.app/superui.css">
