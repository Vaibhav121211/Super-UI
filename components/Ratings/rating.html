Ratings

You can give ratings by using simple Classes from super UI component Lirary . Ratings can give a brief idea of how the product is by just seeing into the rating tab .

Star Ratings

They can be used to give ratings by the user. You can use this by just adding font awesome icons . 


    
<i class="fa fa-2x fa-star"></i>
<i class="fa fa-2x fa-star"></i>
<i class="fa fa-2x fa-star"></i>
<i class="fa fa-2x fa-star"></i>
<i class="far fa-2x fa-star"></i>

Badge Ratings

This is made so that large numbers of customers can get the roughly idea , How good is this product. You can use this badge rating by using ratings--badge class in your code .

5 | 2.2k

    
<div class="rating--badge">
    <p class="badge--container ">5 <i class="fa  fa-star"></i> | 2.2k</p>
</div>
